Our Design Philosophy

Founded in 2012, Nkaji Consultants has grown from a small Nairobi studio to one of East Africa's most sought-after architecture firms. Our name reflects our commitment to thoughtful, context-sensitive design.

We believe buildings should tell the story of their place. Each project begins with deep research into local materials, climate, and cultural traditions, combined with innovative sustainable practices.
